Privacy Notice regarding Grundfos’ marketing services What personal data is collected, for what purpose(s) and on what legal premise? Grundfos will collect the personal data provided by you when signing up for Grundfos marketing services, this can include your first and last name, your email address, your telephone number, your country, your company, related company information, your area of interest and your job role (hereinafter the “Personal Data”). Grundfos will use the Personal Data for the purposes of delivering the requested marketing services to you and to provide you with a personalised experience, information and marketing messages relevant to you via email, SMS, phone, push notifications or letter relating to Grundfos news, tools, applications, products events and promotions. In order for Grundfos to fulfil the above-mentioned activities, Grundfos will store your Personal Data, analyse your behaviour and create a profile based on your actions on the different Grundfos websites, applications or tools, you have access to, including where you have created a Grundfos Account or filled in a form.

To analyse your behaviour Grundfos is using an Adobe Experience Cloud Visitor ID to identify visitors and their interests on the Grundfos websites via cookies. Please, read our cookie policy for more information on Grundfos’ usage of cookies. Additionally, if you have a Grundfos Account, you will get a Public User ID that will be picked up and saved, for each page you have viewed, when you are logged in. Your Adobe Experience Cloud ID or your Public User ID is combined with your email address and from these ID’s Grundfos may utilise additional customer attributes to streamline marketing information that is more relevant to you.

The legal basis for the processing of your Personal Data for newsletter mailings is your consent (Article 6, para. 1, lit. a of the General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) (“GDPR”).

Your Personal Data may only be processed for different purposes if this is needed to comply with legal or regulatory obligations (e.g. relating to court proceedings or criminal prosecution authorities), where you have consented to the respective processing or if the processing is otherwise lawful under applicable law. If processing is for any different purpose we may provide you with additional information. You are not obliged to provide your consent to receive marketing services and therefore the related processing of your personal data. If you do not provide your consent, you will not be able to access any such services.

Your Rights

If you have consented to participate in any personal data processing activities, you may withdraw this consent at any time. Such a request will not affect the legality of any activity undertaken prior to any consent withdrawal. Right of access: You may have the right to obtain from us confirmation as to whether or not personal data concerning you is processed, and, where that is the case, to request access to that personal data. The access information includes – amongst other things – the remit of the processing, the categories of personal data concerned, and the recipients or categories of recipients to whom the personal data has been or will be disclosed to. However, this is not an absolute right and the interests of other individuals may restrict your right of access. You may have the right to obtain a copy of the personal data undergoing processing. For further copies requested by you, we may charge a reasonable fee based on administrative costs. Right to rectification: You may have the right to request us to rectify any inaccurate personal data concerning you. Depending on the purposes of the processing, you may have the right to have incomplete personal data completed, including by means of providing a supplementary statement. Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): Under certain circumstances, you may have the right to obtain from us the deletion of personal data concerning you and we may be obliged to erase such personal data. Right to restriction of processing: Under certain circumstances, you may have the right to obtain from us, restrictions regarding the processing of your personal data. Where this is the case, the respective data will be indicated and may only be processed by us for certain agreed purposes. Right to object: Under certain circumstances, you may have the right to object, on grounds that relate to your individual situation. Or, in the instance where personal data is processed for direct marketing purposes, at any time. In such circumstances, we will no longer process your personal data. Right to data portability: Under certain circumstances, you may have the right to receive the personal data concerning you, which you have provided to us, in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format and you may have the right to transmit this data to a 3rd party without hindrance from us. If you would like to know more or wish to initiate one or more of your above rights, you are welcome to contact Grundfos Holding A/S by sending an email to enews@grundfos.com In case of a complaint, you have the right to lodge any specific complaint with the competent supervisory authority who oversee the processing of your Personal Data.
